# Beta Terms and Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 2026

## Overview

FokalView Resume Readiness Lab is a private beta tool for resume review, ATS keyword matching, and job-fit feedback. The tool provides informational suggestions only. It does not guarantee interviews, offers, employment, legal compliance, applicant tracking system results, or hiring outcomes.

## Beta Access

Access is invite-only and may require a beta access code. FokalView may limit, suspend, or revoke access during the beta.

## Data Processed

To generate analysis, the tool may process:

- Optional email address for cross-device application and progress tracking
- Resume text submitted by the user
- Target role
- Job posting or job tracker context
- Generated analysis, including scores, strengths, improvements, matched keywords, missing keywords, and section notes
- Generated workforce profile data, including skills, tools, work history, education, certifications, projects, career level, and experience estimates

## Beta Retention And Product Improvement

By entering a valid beta access code and using the tool, users agree that FokalView may retain submitted resume text, job context, generated workforce profiles, and analysis outputs during the beta to provide the service, support future review, track workforce-development progress, and improve the product. Retained data may include:

- Target job titles
- Job description keywords
- Matched and missing keywords
- General qualifications
- Skill categories
- Score ranges
- Improvement categories
- Industry or role categories
- Raw resume text submitted for review
- Work history, education, certifications, projects, tools, and career level signals

If a user provides an email address, the system may use it to create a private tracking identifier for linking that user's resumes, workforce profile, and applications over time. Resume and application records use a salted identifier rather than the plain email address.

## De-Identification Limits

Removing direct identifiers reduces risk, but it does not make every record impossible to re-identify. Career history, employers, education, rare skills, project details, or unique job context may still identify a person. Users should not submit information they do not want processed.

## Sensitive Information

Users should not submit Social Security numbers, financial information, government ID numbers, health information, protected class information, passwords, private credentials, or other sensitive personal information.

## Third-Party AI Providers

The tool may send submitted resume and job context to a configured artificial intelligence provider to generate feedback. The configured provider may change over time. API keys are kept server-side and are not intentionally exposed in frontend or browser extension code.

## Security and Retention

FokalView aims to collect only what is needed for the beta, protect retained records, and delete records that are no longer needed. Users may request deletion of retained beta analysis records.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for reviewing AI-generated feedback before relying on it. Users should verify accuracy, avoid adding false experience, and tailor resumes honestly.

## Contact

For access, deletion, or privacy requests, contact FokalView Media.
